Progressive Industry Advisory Company Limited : PIA

 Progressive Industrial Advisory Co., Ltd. or 9advisory was established in 2012. We began as a consultant for cost reduction and increasing efficiency. in the production process. The service was expanded to public transport and freight management by focusing on the operator’s efficiency and passenger satisfaction. We work closely with both government, private company and also internation agency. In the project of public transport for land, rail and marine etc. In the concerning of environmental and rapid technology of electric vehicle (EV). We are stepping to EV business in term of special services for EV eco system as a trader, technical and installation team, EV project feasibility study.


The company's services are divided into 5 parts as follows:
  • Industrial sector
  - - We advise manufacturers, especially SMEs to focus on cost reduction in the production process and logistics management
  • Traffic and transport sector
  - Provide advice on public transport systems to relevant agencies. Both transportation of goods and passenger transport covering road, rail and water transport
  - Conduct traffic and transportation surveys for route design.
  • Research
  - - Set a research question and explore for research answers by working closely with professional team experts from domestic and international universities.
  • Electric Vehicles (EV)
  - Design and install electric work for EV charging station.
  - Sell and maintenance services for EV charging station.
  - Project feasibility for EV charging station.
  • Transport Safety Manager
  - Transportation Safety Manager (TSM) who have been registered by the Department of Land Transport. Ministry of Transport
  - Transportation Safety Manager Trainee (TSM Trainer) who have been registered by the Department of Land Transport. Ministry of Transport
