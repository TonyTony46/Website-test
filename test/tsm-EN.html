TSM


TSM or Transport Safety Management

(TSM) is personnel with specialized knowledge and abilities in safety management and evaluation. who have been trained and registered with the Department of Land Transport

We have people in charge of overseeing transportation safety management at every stage of transportation. and a trainer It has been certified by the Department of Land Transport. To act as transportation safety manager for Freight transport operators and public bus operators According to the Ministry of Transportation Safety Regulations (No. 2) B.E. 2020

The company have 8 training courses for TSM as follows:
